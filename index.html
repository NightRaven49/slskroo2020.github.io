Story

You take a step out of the metallic debris around you, dazed. The last thing you can remember are the blaring alarms from within your landing pod before you passed out from the oxygen deprivation. You wince a little as your grazed feet meet the hard gravel-like ground. It’s a miracle you survived the crash.


Suddenly - something catches your eye. Beyond a small hill, you see billows of smoke and the glow of a fire warming the horizon. After an arduous trek to the top, you are greeted with an impressive sight.


A huge tower looms over a lush valley of green littered with small groups of people surrounding campfires - survivors from your ship covered in blankets and… others? A cheery man sees you and gestures you to join them, to which you apprehensively do so. He offers you a bowl of soup and covers you with a blanket, and a nurse checks you for any severe injuries.


He tells you that their community is made up of people from everywhere in the universe. They’d all come to this planet for various reasons over the past eleven years, from accidental crash-landings to seeking a new life, and your shipwreck has brought the latest batch of newcomers. As you struggle to process all this information, he laughs heartily at your confused face, pats you on the back, and bellows with a warm smile:


"Welcome to Tembusu, my friend!

About

At Tembusu College, we believe that this is a Home of Possibilities. The College houses a whole plethora of resources which students can tap upon for freshmen who walk through our doors to kindle passions or break boundaries – a place of self-discovery. We are a community of vastly unique individuals joined by our pursuits of passion and an institution where play and learn coexist in perfect equilibrium.


With this year marking the eleventh year running for Tembusu College, the theme for this year’s Welcome Week is “New Beginnings” in honour of Tembusu’s rich history and culture from the past decade, while still being a place that provides new and boundless possibilities for students to live their best life during their time here.


Therefore, through this Welcome Week, we wish to inspire students to chart their own journey and create their own legacy.
